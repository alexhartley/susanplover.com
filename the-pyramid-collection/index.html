The Pyramid Collection

The Pyramid Collection was a solo show in The Cultural Quarter of Warrington titled . 'Love is in the Air.'

Each image riffs upon the darker side of romance and relationships building into a full blown narrative flecked with a noire sexual undertone and gallows humour Inspired by personal mythology, the heart of the show is a strong female voice that finally emerges triumphant and free.
