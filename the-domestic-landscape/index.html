The Domestic Landscape

Collage, of a more minimalist style, combines domestic patterns and props with surreal intent.

Often dreamlike in nature and image quality, this series questions perceptions of relationships and unconscious thoughts. Leaning heavily on Magritte and Steampunk, recognisable images are re-contextualised to prompt new narratives.
