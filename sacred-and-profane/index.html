Sacred And Profane

The Sacred and Profane Collection was commisioned for the Revelations Festival in August 2014.

The collages were symbolically nailed to the walls of the Oxford House Gallery in Bethnal Green,London. Each piece responds to the brief but the viewer navigates whether the narrative is a depiction of a Sacred or Profane.

With deliberate recontexturalised religous iconography and subversive borrowing from the art historical, the collection challenges accepted perceptions.
